Theme (if you need one): Innocent but crazy

Collective: Apart from Gary and Ippy this party has no organization. You are invited to join the collective by helping a hand here and there.

Surroundings: We are close to a residential area and in the middle of a beautiful park, so please behave accordingly: no open drug use and try to keep the volume down outside!

No cops = no problems, let’s make sure they don’t come. Most important: always close the venue door behind you! Feel free to volunteer as door watcher! (Cops = 300 euro bond lost, and early closing).

Fire: We have a fire place but there are some clear rules so make sure you know them!

Chillout: There is a nice forest outside, if you bring your hammocks, tarps or other chill items we can make a nice chill out.

Leave no trace! That is: no cigarette buds, no bottle caps on the ground, please use the trash bags and ashtrays. Full trash bag? Take a new one.

Sunday at noon we have to hand back the keys. You are welcome to stay as long as you like, as long as you help us make the deadline! :)

Donations: We will hang a donation QR code. Feel free to donate.
